About the Classic

A Celebration of Community, Fellowship & Jurisdictional Pride

Adelphic Union Lodge #14 is proud to host the inaugural Interstate PHAmily Classic — a landmark event dedicated to community outreach, fellowship, and family fun. This isn't just a sports tournament; it's a holistic community festival celebrating the bonds that make NYC and its surrounding jurisdictions a premier place to live, work, and raise a family.

About the Host

Adelphic Union Lodge #14

"Adelphic Union Lodge #14, under the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of the State of New York, has a storied history of service, brotherhood, and community upliftment. As custodians of Prince Hall Masonic tradition in New York City, the Lodge has been at the forefront of civic engagement, mentorship, and charitable works for generations. The Interstate PHAmily Classic is the next chapter in that legacy — bringing together the NY and NJ Prince Hall Families in a spirit of unity and celebration."

— Adelphic Union Lodge #14, F. & A.M.

Frequently Asked Questions

Riverbank State Park has limited on-site parking. Street parking is available along Riverside Drive. We strongly recommend using public transit — the park is accessible via the 1 train (145th St station) and multiple bus routes (M4, M5, Bx19). Rideshare drop-off is available at the main entrance on 679 Riverside Drive.

Bring comfortable clothing and shoes (you may want to participate in activities), sunscreen, a reusable water bottle, lawn chairs or blankets for outdoor seating, cash or card for vendors, and your best PHAmily spirit! Outside food and beverages are not permitted — food vendors will be on-site.

This is a family event — children of all ages are welcome! Kids 2 and under enter free with a ticketed adult. The Interactive Youth Zone is specifically designed for younger attendees with face painting, inflatables, and workshops. Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

All ticket sales are final — no refunds or exchanges. Tickets may be transferred to another attendee by contacting us at info@phamilyclassic.com prior to the event. In the event of cancellation due to weather or unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, ticket holders will be notified via email regarding next steps.

Not at all! While the athletic showcases feature Masonic jurisdictional teams, the Interstate PHAmily Classic is a community event open to everyone. All are welcome to enjoy the games, entertainment, vendors, and fellowship.

We're welcoming local businesses, artisans, and community organizations! Vendor booth spaces and sponsorship packages are available at multiple tiers. Please fill out the vendor application form below or email us directly at info@phamilyclassic.com for sponsorship decks.

Yes. Riverbank State Park is ADA-accessible with elevator access, accessible restrooms, and designated seating areas. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance so we can ensure your comfort throughout the event.

Riverbank State Park has both indoor and outdoor facilities. In the event of rain, basketball games will move to the indoor gymnasium. The kickball game may be postponed or rescheduled. Updates will be communicated via email and social media. The event is rain or shine — come prepared!

Accessibility Information

Riverbank State Park is fully ADA-accessible. The facility includes elevator access from street level, accessible restrooms on all floors, designated accessible seating areas at both the gymnasium and outdoor fields, and accessible parking spaces in the on-site lot. Service animals are welcome. If you have specific accessibility needs or require accommodations, please contact us at info@phamilyclassic.com at least 48 hours before the event.
